Why choose LMC ?

Transaction taxes are imposed by many crypto projects which goes back to project. Many projects do this to provide long-term funding for their project, especially when the tokens themselves lack real utility. These taxes have a massive impact on the amount of tokens users receive. Limousine coin doesn’t charge any transaction tax at all to ensure the value should be generated from the project itself and not from the community who is trading Limousine coin. we don’t want people to lose money when they enter (or leave) that ecosystem. This shows Limousine coin stands for its community and is here to stay.
